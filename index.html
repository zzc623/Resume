Biography

I am a postdoctoral research fellow in Stanford University, working with Prof. Lei Xing. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in the Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology,Chinese Academy of Sciences, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Yaoqin Xie in June 2019. I was also a visiting scholar in Virginia Tech from 01/2017 to 01/2018. Previously, I received the B.S. from Department of Biomedical Engineering, Zhongshan School of Medicine in Sun Yat-Sen University in 2010.I am dedicated to develop advanced machine-learning based algorithms on medical imaging and medical image analysis, with a primary focus on medical images.
